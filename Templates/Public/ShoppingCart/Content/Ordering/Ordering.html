What's an IMEI number?

IMEI or 'International Mobile Equipment Identity' is a unique 15-digit number assigned to all cellular devices. IMEI carry information on mobile phones are concerned such as mobile phone manufacturer, phone model, and so on. You can check the IMEI of your phone by pressing a combination * # 06 # on the call button.
